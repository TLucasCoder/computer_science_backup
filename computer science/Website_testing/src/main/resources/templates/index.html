About Us

Hong Kong Public Affairs and Social Service Society (HKPASS), first founded in LSE, is a non-profit student-led organization. Until now, the HKPASS family has expanded to over 20 universities in the UK. Soton HKPASS joined University of Southampton Studentsâ€™ Union (SUSU) back in 2014. Aimed at enhancing social awareness and community involvement of students, as well as promoting the importance of public concerns regarding social, economic and political issues worldwide, we hold a variety of internal and external social events throughout the year. Soton HKPASS aspires to help freshers fully adapt to UK university life, and is determined to create a delightful university experience to students.
